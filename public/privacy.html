Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Prodvote ("we", "our", "us") is operated by LitsAI Technologies. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Prodvote mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Prodvote:

  • Account information: Email address, username, and password (encrypted) when you create an account.
  • Profile data: Your subscription tier, coins balance, badges, and activity within the app.
  • User-generated content: Feature requests, votes, comments, and feedback you submit.
  • Usage data: General app usage patterns to improve the service.
  • Purchase data: Subscription and in-app purchase information processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Prodvote service
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To process in-app purchases and coin transactions
  • To send important service updates and notifications
  • To display your username and tier badge alongside your contributions
  • To improve and develop new features

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Supabase: Our database and authentication provider
  • RevenueCat: For managing subscriptions and purchases
  • Apple/Google: For processing payments through their respective stores
  • Resend: For sending transactional emails

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), secure password hashing, and row-level security policies on our database. Your payment information is handled entirely by Apple, Google, or RevenueCat and is never stored on our servers.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access and update your profile information at any time
  • Delete your account by contacting us
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

6. Children's Privacy

Prodvote is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.
